doc3 = nlp(u'Can I please have 500 dollars of Microsoft stock?')
nlp(...): When you pass the string to the nlp object, SpaCy processes the text using its language model. This process includes:

Tokenization: Breaking the text into individual words or tokens.
Part-of-Speech (POS) Tagging: Assigning parts of speech to each token, like noun, verb, adjective, etc.
Dependency Parsing: Analyzing the grammatical structure of the sentence.
Named Entity Recognition (NER): Identifying entities in the text, such as "Microsoft" being recognized as an organization.
